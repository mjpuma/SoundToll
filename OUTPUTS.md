## period_comparison.png

**Caption:** Pre-plague (1705–1708) vs post-plague (1710–1713) network comparison. Top left: number of ports (nodes). Top right: number of routes (edges). Bottom left: total passages. Bottom right: network density. Post-plague period shows fewer ports and routes but higher density, consistent with a more concentrated network after the 1709 Baltic plague. Data: Sound Toll Registers Online (STRO).
